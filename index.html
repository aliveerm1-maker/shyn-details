Our Story

Built With Purpose

In 2025, two Houston teenagers — Aliveer Merchant and Aaden Dickson — decided to build something real.

We saw an opportunity to provide a service Houston actually needs: a reliable, professional mobile detailing team that comes to you, does the job right, and treats every car like it matters. No shortcuts. No half-done work.

We started Shyn Details because we believed in the value of honest hustle — showing up, working hard, and delivering results that speak for themselves. Houston deserves better than average, and that's exactly what we bring.

We are a dedicated team of teen entrepreneurs who take immense pride in our work and guarantee professional results on every job.

⚡ Fast & Efficient Each full service takes 1–3 hours. We value your time.
